KURUKSHETRA UNIVERSITY
Department of computer science and applications

in the year 1981 the department of computer science and applications was established and kurukshetra university was the first in the country to start a UGC supported one year MCA programme. this was replaced by two year mca programme from the academic year 1984-85 onwards. in december 1990, UGC sanctioned the proposal of department of computer science and applications to update the two-year MCA programme to three-year mca programme.

Library

The department library provides approximately 10k books (text and reference) with a sitting capacity of 60 students.
